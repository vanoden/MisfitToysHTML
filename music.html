The Music

The Misfit Toys Band plays a wide variety of music from the 60s to today. We play a classic and alternative rock leaning sometimes into progressive hits. These include songs with an edge and a lot of energy that are sometimes more difficult to play and not as often covered. Our goal is to play music that is a little more challenging and interesting but still available and attention grabbing. We are always adding new songs to our set list, so check back often to see what we've added.
