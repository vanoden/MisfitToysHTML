The Band

The Misfit Toys are an Eastern Massachusetts based cover band performing Alternative Rock, Pop Rock, Punk Rock, Classic Rock and Hard Rock hits primarily from the 80's and 90's. We are fully equipped to provide sound for venues from porches to clubs to event halls.

Dave Button Drums and Percussion

Dave is a lifelong drummer with influences from some of the top rock, progressive and alternative stickmen in the game. He loves providing timing, rhythm and energy from the back to drive the band forward.

Tony Caravello Lead Guitar and Vocals

Tony has been performing in bands since he started in college playing alternative rock and metal in the early 90s. He is not your usual frontman, but enjoys entertaining a crowd, singing, playing guitar and occasionally some harmonica or keyboards.

Joe Croscup Bass and Vocals

Joe Croscup hails from The Kirk in upstate New York and lives in Dedham. He brings the low end groove and high, edgy vocals.

Oz Guitar and Vocals

Oz is the latest addition to the Misfit Toys. Bringing a solid rhythm and daring leads he adds power and professionalism to the band.

Joe Hart Guitar and Bass

Joe Hart is a founding member of The Misfit Toys and a professional musician as the lead member of The Joe Hart Band. He plays and teaches guitar at Band Gig in Dedham.
